How does a parking guidance system work?

Understand the technology behind the solution.

Detection

Detection can be done by installing a detector above each parking place, pointing a camera to an individual or multiple parking spaces or placing a sensors at the entrance/exit of the parking garage. In the case of detectors and cameras, developed specifically for guidance purposes, it constantly checks the presence of a vehicle and transmits the situation to the parking software.

Software

The software provides real time reporting, statistics for individual parking spaces and the status for each of the SP components, ensuring an efficient management of the car park.

Indication

Thanks to the information supplied by the software, the dynamic displays located at strategic points of the car park provide, in real time, the required information to guide customers to an aisle with free spaces.

Benefits

After all is said and done your team and your customers can enjoy the many benefits of having this system installed. Parking statistics and reports can help operators check their numbers for accuracy and institutions decide when it is time to build another parking garage. Your customers can find a parking sapce more easlily.
