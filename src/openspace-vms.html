Open Space VMS

The Parking Logix OpenSpace single, multi-level & multi lot parking solutions include the OpenSpace Variable Messaging Sign (VMS). This sign communicates wirelessly with OpenSpace using our Data Collector to display real-time occupancy to drivers.

Features

  • Wireless connectivity
  • Energy efficient
  • Durable and long lasting
  • Remote control
  • Quick and easy sign management
  • Brilliant visibility
