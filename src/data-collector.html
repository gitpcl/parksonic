Counting Solutions

Smart sensors embedded into speed humps or directly into road surfaces detect and count each vehicle that enters or exits your lot. The sensors communicate wirelessly to data collectors and signs to display real time occupancy to parkers.

Features

  • Portable
  • Energy efficient
  • Sets up in minutes
  • Wireless connectivity
  • Durable and long lasting

Options

  • Universal mounting bracket
  • Solar power/AC/battery power
  • Web-enabled

Specs
